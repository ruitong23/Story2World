# Story2World

Turn Any Story Into A Living World.

From novels, scripts, and lore documents, it automatically builds structured world knowledge, agent-ready characters, and dynamic simulations.

Story2World automatically extracts and models:

- Characters
- Relationships
- Locations
- Events
- Items
- World Rules

and eventually allow AI agents to continue the world beyond the original narrative.

## What Can Story2World Do?
### Build a Living World

Extract characters, locations, events, items, organizations, and world rules from long-form stories.

### Generate Agent Profiles

Convert story characters into agent-ready profiles with memory, goals, relationships, and knowledge.

### Simulate Alternative Timelines

Start from any point in the story and explore how the world evolves when characters make new decisions.

### Analyze Complex Narratives

Build relationship graphs, event timelines, and world models for large fictional universes.


## Why Story2World?

Most story AI systems focus on question answering.

Story2World focuses on world reconstruction.

Instead of asking:

"Who is Sun Wukong?"

Story2World builds:

- The character
- His relationships
- His memories
- His goals
- His abilities
- His place in the world

The goal is not to answer questions about a story.

The goal is to recreate the world itself.
